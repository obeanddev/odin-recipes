Zucchini Gratin

Description

A classic of summer cuisine, this gratin contains zucchini and Bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchini
  • Pepper to taste
  • 250ml milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 ounces of grated cheese

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan.
  2. Introduce the flour little by little while cooking for a minute.
  3. Add gradually the milk, continuing to cook.
  4. Stir constantly until thich enough.
  5. Preheat the oven at 200° Celsius (About 390° Farenheit).
  6. Slice the zucchini in quarter of inch rounds.
  7. Cook the zucchini in a frying pan until brown.
  8. Put the zucchini in a gratin dish.
  9. Add the pepper if you want.
  10. Cover with the sauce prepared with milk and flour.
  11. Top with grated cheese.
  12. Put in the oven and cook for at least half an hour.
